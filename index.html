Qualifying Earnings Band?When enabled, employer contributions are calculated on qualifying earnings only (lower and upper limits vary by tax year) — not your full salary. Common for auto-enrolment schemes. Most tech employers use full salary, so leave this off unless you know otherwise.
Employer NI Pass-through?When you sacrifice salary, your employer also saves on their NI. Some employers pass part or all of that saving into your pension. Check your scheme details — this is free money if offered.
Scottish Taxpayer?Scotland has different income tax bands and rates (19%–48%). If you live in Scotland, enable this for accurate calculations. NI rates are the same across the UK.
